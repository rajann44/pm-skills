# Validation Scenario 7: Ambiguous Diagnostic Response

This artifact represents the diagnostic routing output when faced with ambiguous user requests (e.g., "Help me with my product, things feel off").

---

## Strategic Diagnostic Approach

When user intent is ambiguous, dumping product frameworks prematurely is a hard anti-pattern. We must ask targeted diagnostic questions across the four pillars of product management to isolate the primary friction point.

### Diagnostic Questions

To help isolate why things "feel off," please select the statement below that best describes your current situation:

1.  **"Our growth has slowed, or we aren't sure if we have reached Product-Market Fit."**
    *   *Diagnostic check:* We need to analyze your cohort retention curves and North Star metric.
    *   *Next Step:* Load `../product-management/references/metrics-growth.md` to run cohort and retention analysis.
2.  **"We are shipping a lot of features, but they don't seem to move our business metrics."**
    *   *Diagnostic check:* Your team might be trapped in a "feature factory" or shipping without strategic focus.
    *   *Next Step:* Load `../product-management/references/strategy.md` to define your ICP and DHM models, and `../product-management/references/conflicts.md#2-team-charter-empowered-teams-vs-feature-teams` to check your team charter.
3.  **"We aren't sure what features to build next, or we keep arguing about our roadmap."**
    *   *Diagnostic check:* You need a continuous customer interviewing rhythm and opportunity mapping.
    *   *Next Step:* Load `../product-management/references/discovery.md` to map opportunities (OST) and draft Mom-Test customer interview guides.
4.  **"Our projects are taking too long, engineers are frustrated by changing specs, or our backlog is unmanageable."**
    *   *Diagnostic check:* Your delivery cycle, estimation, and backlog grooming processes are bottlenecked.
    *   *Next Step:* Load `../product-management/references/execution.md` to apply Shape Up cycle planning, appetite scoping, and backlog elimination.
